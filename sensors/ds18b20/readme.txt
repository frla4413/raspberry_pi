This is a digital sensor that can read the temperature.
Tutorial:
https://www.circuitbasics.com/raspberry-pi-ds18b20-temperature-sensor-tutorial/

Wiering:
  o VCC (RED) to  3.3V
  o GND (BLACK) to GNDand
  o DATA (YELLOW) to GPIO4
  o 10kOhm resistor between VCC and DATA

Works when measuring the room temperature and the temperature in a galss of cold water.
